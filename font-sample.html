What ever happened to Miss Mary Mack?

Do you remember Miss Mary Mack?

Once a figure on school playgrounds, her name could be heard being sung by school children.

"Miss Mary Mack. All dressed in Black. With silver Buttons. All down her Back."

Later in the song, the children would sing, "She jumped so High. She reached the Sky. And she nevercame Back Until the 4th of July.

But has anyone seen her? Has Miss Mary Mack every really came back?
